Bio

Austin Richard Post, known professionally as Post Malone, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter.

When Post was 9, he moved to Grapevine, Texas with his father and stepmother Jodie. After his father became the manager of concessions for the Dallas Cowboys. Post began to play the guitar and auditioned for the band Crown the Empire in 2010 but was rejected after his guitar strings broke at the audition. His interest in learning playing guitar was from the popular video game Guitar Hero. Post always loved for the genre emo music, and appeared for a DJ set at Emo Nite in Los Angeles. His very first foray into professional music began when he was in a heavy metal band. Soon after, he says he switched to softer rock then to hip-hop, before beginning to experiment on FL Studio which i have used. After moving to LA, Post, and 7 other producers and artists formed the music group BLCKVRD.

7 members of the group, and Post, moved into a house in San Fernando Valley together. while living Here Austin met 1st Down of FKi. He met 1st and Rich from FKi and Rex Kudo who produced 7 of Posts tracks, including "White Iverson". Post recorded the song two days after writing it. "White Iverson" is, in part, a reference to the professional basketball player Allen Iverson. In February 2015, upon completion, it was uploaded to Post's SoundCloud account. On July 19, 2015, Post released a music video for "White Iverson". The single received praise from Mac Miller and Wiz Khalifa. However, the song was notoriously mocked by Earl Sweatshirt.
